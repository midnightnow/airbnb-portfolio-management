## Business Model Summary

### Target Client
- **Portfolio Size:** 5-10 Airbnb properties
- **Annual Revenue:** $180K-$360K from STR portfolio
- **Current Pain:** 15-25 hours/week managing properties, suboptimal pricing
- **Decision Driver:** Time vs. money trade-off (values their time at $100-200/hr)

### Value Proposition
> "We deliver institutional-grade technology and white-glove service at 60% lower cost than hiring in-house staff. Clients reclaim 15-25 hours/week while typically seeing 10-15% revenue increases through dynamic pricing and operational excellence."

### Economics (5 Clients, 50 Properties)
- **Monthly Revenue:** $27,000
- **Monthly Costs:** $6,550 (tech + labor)  
- **Monthly Profit:** $20,450
- **Profit Margin:** 76%
- **Annual Profit:** $245,400

### Acquisition Strategy
1. **B2B Partnerships** (70% of clients) - Wealth advisors, attorneys, brokers
2. **LinkedIn Outreach** (20% of clients) - Account-based marketing
3. **Referrals** (10% of clients) - From existing happy clients

### Competitive Differentiation
- **vs. Traditional Managers (25-30% fees):** 40% cheaper with better tech
- **vs. Budget Services (8-12% fees):** Full management, not just tools
- **vs. DIY:** Reclaim 20+ hours/week + revenue optimization
